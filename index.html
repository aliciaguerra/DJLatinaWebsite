DJ Latina gets booked to DJ weddings, music festivals, parades, conventions, and corporate events across Southern California. She spins everything from Latin, top 40, hip-hop/R&B, house, oldies, funk/disco, rock, and country.

NIGHTCLUB DJ

DJ Latina is an experienced nightclub DJ. Club deejaying is a completely different animal from mobile deejaying; there's a larger focus on technical skill. DJ Latina embraces club deejaying as a creative outlet and employs the following techniques to enhance her performance:

  • Beatmatching
  • Turntablism (Scratching & Beat Juggling)
  • Creative Transitions (Tone Play, Word Play)
  • Custom Redrums, Bootlegs, Mashups

FAQ

How far advance do I have to book DJ Latina?

Whether your event is two years or two days down the line, try giving DJ Latina a call. If your date is open on her calendar, she'll happily take it on.

What is the process to book DJ Latina?

DJ Latina offers a free over-the-phone or in-person consultation to discuss your event details, event vision, preferences, and audio-visual needs. She is very accomodating to and willing to discuss specific requests.
For large projects and events, she visits the venue in person.
If required by the venue, DJ Latina does have liability insurance and can provide them with a certificate.

Does DJ Latina charge for setup and breakdown?

No. Never. No matter how long it takes, you'll never have setup and breakdown time added to your bill.
