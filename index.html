Recording Engineer

While modern recording software enables musicians to make their own home recordings, these rarely compare in quality to the work of a well-trained audio engineer. DJ Latina is a Pro Tools certified recording engineer who is well-aquainted with SSL mixing consoles and can run sessions quickly and effectively.

She implements the following audio recording techniques:

  • Leveraging Mic Polar Patterns
  • Metering
  • Pre-production/Pre-recording
  • Room Treatment & Determining Ideal Mic Placement

Mixing/Mastering Engineer

Send DJ Latina your raw recorded tracks and get back a professional and great-sounding mix. Up to three revisions are included for each track.

Some of the mixing/mastering techniques DJ Latina may employ include:

  • Stereo Widening
  • Dynamics Control (Compression)
  • Dynamic Range Check (Metering)
  • Using Inverse EQ to Clear Space in a Mix

FAQ

How can I hire DJ Latina for online mixing and mastering services?

For competitively-priced online mixing services, simply send DJ Latina your raw recorded tracks and get back a professional sounding mix. The service includes a professional mix and up to 3 revisions.
DJ Latina's mastering service enhances the loudness and punch of your mix, while maintaining clear and nuanced sound. The service includes a professional master, up to 3 revisions, commercial loudness, and fading and transitions.

What is the process to book DJ Latina?

DJ Latina offers a free over-the-phone or in-person consultation to discuss your event details, event vision, preferences, and audio-visual needs. She is very accomodating to and willing to discuss specific requests.
For large projects and events, she visits the venue in person.
If required by the venue, DJ Latina does have liability insurance and can provide them with a certificate.

Does DJ Latina charge for setup and breakdown?

No. Never. No matter how long it takes, you'll never have setup and breakdown time added to your bill.
