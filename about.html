Hi, I'm Alicia, AKA DJ Latina.

I'm a 26-year-old Mexican-American female DJ based in San Diego, CA.

I have a musical background that dates back to early childhood. My mother is an accomplished singer, guitarist, accordionist, and pianist who currently leads her church's worship ensemble. She enrolled me in music lessons as soon as she was able to. So, throughout my childhood and adolescene, I played in marching bands, pep bands, orchestras, mariachi bands, guitar ensembles, and drum circles. I play several musical instruments including but not limited to the Bb clarinet, guitar, and drums.
